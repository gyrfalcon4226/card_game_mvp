Card Battle Game

Welcome to the Card Battle Game! Here are the basic rules:

  • Each player draws 5 cards from a deck of cards.
  • Each card has an attack and health value, and a special suit effect.
  • During each turn, both players select one card to battle.
  • The card with higher attack value reduces the opponent's card health.
  • If a card's health drops to zero or below, it is removed from play.
  • Special suit effects:
    • Spade: Counterattack - Deals half of its attack as damage back to the attacker.
    • Heart: Lifesteal - Heals for half of its attack when it deals damage.
    • Diamond: Shield - Gains shield equal to half of its attack each turn.
    • Club: Poison - Deals half of its attack as poison damage over two turns.
  • The game ends when one player has no more cards left.
